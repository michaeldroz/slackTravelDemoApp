HAPPY TOURS

We offer the following tours below. If you don't see something you are looking for , please ask us. We are happy to make recommendations to other tour providers.

Budapest Bike Tour


Tour around Budapest with us on our well maintained urban assualt bikes. The max group size is 12. We can accomodate uto to 6 rous a day. this is an easy to moderate bike ride arond the heart of Budapest. You will see important cultural and historical sites. We also stop in the Central Bar District for a quick refresher.

Old Town Walking Tour


Enjoy a Budapest tour with a local guide who is eager to show you the city's grand sights and its simple pleasures - such as sharing a bowl of goulash with friends.

Danube River Tour


For boat tours in the Danube Delta we use medium sized motro boats for up to 25 persons, specially designed for wildife observation, often with an elevated deck. The advantage of these boats lies in their ability to navigate extremely shallow waters so, no matter what the season almost any part of the delta can be explored.
